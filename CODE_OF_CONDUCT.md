# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political attacks, publishing others' private information, and other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers via the contact in the repository profile. Maintainers will review and investigate, and respond in a way they deem appropriate.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
